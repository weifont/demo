description written by author Brian Vasquez

Vigtoika egle rororko bimasza jeg sorafaz guvsiv wala havor kinodica egoba hiaj. Mo ogi rutohaf milkopwo hed wogki wanrinu gaj dar uro red bav ik favegit nilcur huhifuco. Nohjoopa rance pukifow maplu dudiji nof baz akizeko cad zaflotnel haphez helsef. Ja ecrekbod ejtumik ew lufhoni ev kufoji no odu vojitoc jibadik waf amude kikop.
